Google Drive: Daily Photo Upload

For FCs and CCs. 10 photos per day to the shared Drive folder.

!10 photos per day. Both FCs and CCs upload to their respective folder. Use Day X.Y naming.
01
Where to Upload
RootFC Deliverables
→
FolderPhotos
→
Your FolderFC or CC
02
Naming Convention

Every file: Day [camp day].[photo number]

Camp DayPhoto Files
Day 0Day 0.1, Day 0.2 ... Day 0.10
Day 1Day 1.1, Day 1.2 ... Day 1.10
Day 2Day 2.1, Day 2.2 ... Day 2.10
......
Day 10Day 10.1, Day 10.2 ... Day 10.10
03
Rules
Upload exactly 10 photos every camp day
Name each file as Day X.Y (no random names like IMG_001)
Upload to the correct folder (FC or CC)
Upload on the same day. Don't batch multiple days.
Day numbering matches your QCR form camp day (Day 0 to Day 10)
